Hummus Recipe

Sumptious bowl of Hummus

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (Garbanzo beans)
  • Tahini Paste
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Water (or chickpea cooking liquid)
  • Parsley

Steps

  1. Rinse and drain one can (or about 1½ cups cooked) of chickpeas.
  2. In a food processor, add chickpeas, 2–3 tablespoons tahini, 1–2 cloves garlic, and juice of 1 lemon.
  3. Blend for about 30 seconds to start breaking down the ingredients.
  4. Scrape down the sides of the bowl, then add 2 tablespoons olive oil and a pinch of salt.
  5. Blend again while slowly adding a few tablespoons of water (or chickpea liquid) until smooth and creamy.
  6. Taste and adjust: add more lemon juice, salt, or tahini if needed.
  7. Transfer to a bowl and drizzle with olive oil.
  8. Garnish with paprika, cumin, or chopped parsley if desired.
  9. Serve with pita bread, vegetables, or use as a spread.
