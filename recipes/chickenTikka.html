Chicken Tikka Recipe

Exquisite Chicken Tikka

Ingredients

  • Boneless chicken pieces (preferably thighs or breast)
  • Yogurt
  • Ginger-garlic paste
  • Lemon juice
  • Red chili powder
  • Turmeric powder
  • Cumin powder
  • Coriander powder
  • Garam masala
  • Salt
  • Cooking oil or melted butter
  • Fresh coriander (for garnish)
  • Onion slices and lemon wedges (for serving)

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken into bite-sized cubes and set aside.
  2. In a bowl, mix yogurt, ginger-garlic paste, lemon juice, and all the dry spices (chili powder, turmeric, cumin, coriander, garam masala, and salt).
  3. Add a tablespoon of oil or melted butter to the marinade for richness.
  4. Add the chicken pieces and mix well to coat them evenly.
  5. Cover the bowl and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 1–2 hours (or overnight for deeper flavor).
  6. Preheat your grill, oven, or pan to medium-high heat.
  7. Thread the marinated chicken pieces onto skewers (if using) or place them directly on a greased tray/pan.
  8. Grill or bake until the chicken is cooked through and slightly charred — about 15–20 minutes, turning occasionally.
  9. Brush lightly with oil or butter during cooking for extra juiciness.
  10. Remove from heat, garnish with coriander, and serve hot with onion slices and lemon wedges.
